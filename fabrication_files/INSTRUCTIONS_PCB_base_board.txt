PCB STACKUP
Any 1.6mm thick, 4-layer FR4 stackup is acceptable.

PCB SPECIFICATIONS
Material:
Any standard FR4, regular temperature grade

Surface finish:
Any RoHS compliant surface finish, ENIG preferable

Solder mask:
Black/Blue/Green

Silk screen:
White

Solder paste/profile:
Any RoHS compliant


ASSEMBLY NOTES
Assembled PCBs must not be cleaned with a solvent or washed. PCBs contain MEMS sensor(s).
